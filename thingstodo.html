Nearby Attractions

There are plenty of places to check out near the venue.

  • Shenandoah National Park and Skyline Drive – many scenic overlooks and trailheads for hiking trails of varying levels of difficulty.
  • – we recommend reserving a tour time in advance.
  • Route 151 in Nelson County – a popular destination for multiple wineries and breweries plus a cidery, distillery, meadery, and several restaurants (desginate a driver or take a shuttle!)
  • Monticello Wine Trail – you can do tastings at two or three wineries in an afternoon or buy a glass of wine and chill out on the patio at many of these wineries (again, please designate a driver!)
  • Humpback Rocks – a short, very popular hike to a rock outcrop with outstanding views of the Blue Ridge and the Shenandoah Valley; accessed via the Blue Ridge Parkway
  • Crabtree Falls – a moderate, popular hike alongside a series of waterfalls
  • Old Rag – a long, strenuous but also very popular hike with great views and a few geological curiosities
  • Starr Hill Brewery in Crozet – follow up a hike or a brewery tour with lunch at Crozet Pizza

Restaurants

There are plenty of places to eat near the venue.

  • Bang – Cozy house with stylish decor & backlit bar preparing Asian-inspired tapas & inventive martinis.
  • Citizen Burger Bar – Inviting eatery turning out hamburgers & many craft beers in a brick-walled space with a patio.
  • Mono Loco – Twinkling lights adorn a funky cantina known for contemporary Latin fare & specialty drinks.
  • The Whiskey Jar – Classic Southern dishes from local ingredients, served in comfy digs with lots of reclaimed touches.
  • Mas – Buzzy Spanish eatery offering tapas & wine in an industrial-chic setting with a lively bar & patio.
  • The Alley Light – Offers French small plates, boards & shareable dishes, with a handsome bar for cocktails & wine.
  • Ace Biscuit & Barbecue – Humble joint with all-day breakfast biscuit sandwiches & BBQ, plus local beer & from-scratch sauces.
  • Bodo's Bagels – One of a local trio of New York-style bagel shops offering counter-serve breakfast & sandwiches.
